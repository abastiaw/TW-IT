About US

Specialist in assisting companies that appreciate employees as an asset to be more confident in their work, building and establishing a good corporate image in the eyes of customers through a corporate uniform.

PT. Teratai Widjaja offers a complete services in the whole process; from customized design, selection of thematic fabrics, materials and accessories, product sample preparation, real production running, packing to on-time delivery schedule.

Our banker has given credence to us by providing a continuous financial support for more than 16 years.

PT. Teratai Widjaja is fully committed. We guarantee the quality of our products in accordance with customer's requirement.

PT. Teratai Widjaja also houses two brands, namely Moslemate Shannindya and Stepanka Laterai. Moslemate Shannindya is more towards Muslim fashion brands, while Stepanka Laterai is like its parent.

PT. Teratai Widjaja but can buy in smaller quantities selling daily uniforms, hospital uniforms and security uniforms

Scope of Work

1. Desgin


Our designer will create and develop the design as requested

2. Material Sourcing


We give suggestion to use variety kinds of material and fabric and choose the most suitable quality as well as budget

3. Sample Development


A prototype sample will be developed based on the design and chosen material until approved by customer

4. Measurement


This is the important process in our production cycles. The method is done by fitting (tester) sample or measuring individual body size. We've been developing team and system in handling this particular process

5. Production


All our team will do a final check regarding details of working process mentioned above, prior to cutting as a starting point of production process

6. Quality Control


We do a complete check of quality that effect a garment performance throughly from cutting, numbering, interlining pressing, stitching until finishing and packing.

7. Setting & Packing


As every employee should receive right uniform where ever their location and department, to do setting and packing is not an easy one. We develop a system that could minimize and avoid packing errors.

8. Distribution


We make sure that the uniform is delivered to the right place and person, and collect documents of receipt or evident for invoicing purpose to fulfill customer's administration requirements. The status of delivery will be informed to customer on an up to date basis.

9. After Sales Service


As uniform is handmade and very personal, we are pleased to take care and solve each personal complain.

10. Guarantee


We provide 6 months guarantee to repair and or replace any defective garments caused by the production fault.
